Chef's Specials

Discover Our Chef's Specials

Signature Curry

Golden Spoon’s Signature Curry is the heart of our menu — a dish that brings comfort and flavor to every table. Cooked slowly with hand-ground spices, fresh herbs, and tender cuts of meat or seasonal vegetables, it delivers a perfect balance of richness and warmth. Each spoonful carries the aroma of traditional Indian kitchens, blending authenticity with modern finesse.

Prepared with a creamy base of tomatoes, cashews, and a hint of saffron, our Signature Curry is served alongside soft butter naan or fragrant basmati rice. The dish is not only filling but also a celebration of flavors that linger long after your meal. For those seeking the true essence of Indian cuisine, this curry is a must-try experience at Golden Spoon.

Wood-Fired Pizza

Our Wood-Fired Pizza is baked to perfection in a traditional stone oven, giving it a smoky crust and irresistible flavor. Each pizza is crafted with freshly kneaded dough, house-made sauces, and the finest mozzarella, ensuring every bite is light, crispy, and bursting with flavor.

From the simplicity of a classic Margherita to the boldness of Pepperoni or Veggie Supreme, our pizzas bring the best of Italy to your plate. Fresh basil, olive oil drizzles, and seasonal toppings add to the experience, making it a perfect meal for friends, family, or even a solo indulgence. At Golden Spoon, pizza is not just food — it’s an art form baked with love.

Asian Fusion Bowl

The Asian Fusion Bowl is our creative take on modern Asian dining, combining noodles, stir-fried vegetables, and your choice of chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Tossed in a flavorful soy-ginger sauce, the bowl delivers a balance of savory, sweet, and spicy notes that awaken your taste buds.

Each ingredient is freshly prepared to maintain crunch and texture, while sesame seeds, coriander, and chili flakes provide the perfect finishing touch. Healthy yet hearty, this bowl is ideal for those seeking a satisfying meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor. It’s a favorite among our guests who love global cuisine with a wholesome twist.

Chef’s Seafood Platter

For seafood lovers, Golden Spoon proudly presents the Chef’s Seafood Platter, a true ocean-inspired delight. Featuring buttery lobster, smoky grilled prawns, and crispy calamari, this platter captures the freshness of the sea in every bite. Served with lemon butter sauce, garlic bread, and seasonal sides, it is designed to be both indulgent and shareable.

Our chefs carefully source seafood from trusted markets to ensure the highest quality and freshness. Whether enjoyed as a centerpiece for a family dinner or paired with fine wine for a romantic evening, the Seafood Platter promises an unforgettable dining experience. It’s the ultimate way to celebrate food and togetherness at Golden Spoon.

Dessert Delight

No meal at Golden Spoon is complete without our Dessert Delight collection. From the rich layers of classic Tiramisu to the soft, syrup-soaked warmth of Gulab Jamun, our desserts are designed to bring happiness with every bite. Each sweet dish is handcrafted daily to ensure freshness and authenticity.

We also feature seasonal fruit cheesecakes, chocolate lava cakes, and ice cream combinations that offer a variety of flavors for every mood. Whether you’re a fan of creamy, chocolatey, or fruity desserts, Golden Spoon’s Dessert Delight is the perfect way to end your dining experience on a sweet note.
